Everything Except the Weather

新概念英语第2册 · 第 38 课 · 中英文对照课文

▶ 点读 / 听力练习
  1. Lesson 38第38课
  2. Everything Except the Weather除了天气一切都好
  3. Why did Harrison sell his house so quickly?为什么哈里森这么快就卖掉了他的房子？
  4. My old friend, Harrison, had lived in the Mediterranean for many years before he returned to England.我的老朋友哈里森曾经住在这里。在地中海地区生活了许多年在他回到英格兰之前。
  5. He had often dreamed of retiring in England and had planned to settle down in the country.他经常梦想着在英格兰退休。并且计划在乡下安顿下来
  6. He had no sooner returned than he bought a house and went to live there.他一回来就买了一栋房子并搬去那里住
  7. Almost immediately he began to complain about the weather, for even though it was still summer, it rained continually and it was often bitterly cold.几乎立刻，他就开始抱怨起来。抱怨天气因为尽管还是夏天雨一直下个不停，天气也常常非常寒冷。
  8. After so many years of sunshine,在享受了多年阳光之后
  9. Harrison got a shock.哈里森感到非常震惊。
  10. He acted as if he had never lived in England before.他的举止就好像他以前从未在英国生活过一样。
  11. In the end, it was more than he could bear.最终，这超出了他的承受能力。
  12. He had hardly had time to settle down when he sold the house and left the country.他几乎还没来得及安顿下来呢。就卖掉了房子离开了这个国家
  13. The dream he had had for so many years ended there.他怀揣多年的梦想就此破灭
  14. Harrison had thought of everything except the weather.哈里森考虑到了所有可能的因素，唯独没有考虑到天气。
