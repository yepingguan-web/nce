Never Too Old To Learn

新概念英语第2册 · 第 85 课 · 中英文对照课文

▶ 点读 / 听力练习
  1. Lesson 85第85课
  2. Never Too Old To Learn活到老学到老
  3. How long has Mr. Page been teaching?佩奇先生教书已经有多久了？
  4. I have just received a letter from my old school, informing me that my former headmaster, Mr. Stuart Page, will be retiring next week.我刚刚收到我母校寄来的一封信，信中告诉我，我的前任校长斯图尔特·佩奇先生将于下周退休。
  5. Pupils of the school old and new, will be sending him a present to mark the occasion.学校的历届学生都会送他一份礼物来纪念这个时刻。
  6. All those who have contributed towards the gift will sign their names in a large album which will be sent to the headmaster's home.所有为这份礼物出过力的人都会在一本大纪念册上签名，这本纪念册将被送到校长家中。
  7. We shall all remember Mr. Page for his patience and understanding and for the kindly encouragement he gave us when we went so unwillingly to school.我们都会永远铭记佩奇先生——他总是充满耐心与理解，每当我们极不情愿地去上学时，他总会给予我们鼓励与支持。
  8. A great many former pupils will be attending a farewell dinner in his honour next Thursday.许多他的老同学将会参加下周四为他举办的告别晚宴。
  9. It is a curious coincidence that the day before his retirement, Mr. Page will have been teaching for a total of forty years.巧合的是，在佩奇先生退休的前一天，他教书正好满四十年。
  10. After he has retired he will devote himself to gardening.退休后，他将全身心投入到园艺活动中去。
  11. For him, this will be an entirely new hobby.对他来说，这将是一个全新的爱好。
  12. But this does not matter, for, as he has often remarked, one is never too old to learn.但这并不重要，因为正如他经常说的那样：人永远都不应该停止学习。
