Always Young

新概念英语第2册 · 第 17 课 · 中英文对照课文

▶ 点读 / 听力练习
  1. Lesson 17第17课
  2. Always Young青春常驻
  3. Why doesn't Aunt Jennifer tell anyone how old she is?为什么詹妮弗阿姨从不告诉别人她的真实年龄呢？
  4. My aunt Jennifer is an actress.我的阿姨詹妮弗是一名演员。
  5. She must be at least thirty-five years old.她至少有三十五岁了。
  6. In spite of this, she often appears on the stage as a young girl.尽管如此，她仍然经常以年轻女孩的形象出现在舞台上。
  7. Jennifer will have to take part in a new play soon.詹妮弗很快将出演一部新剧。
  8. This time, she will be a girl of seventeen.这一次，她将是一个十七岁的女孩。
  9. In the play, she must appear in a bright red dress and long black stockings.在剧中，她必须穿着鲜红色的连衣裙和黑色长筒袜出场。
  10. Last year in another play, she had to wear short socks and a bright, orange-coloured dress.去年，在另一部戏剧中……她不得不穿短袜和一条鲜艳的橙色连衣裙。
  11. If anyone ever asks her how old she is, she always answers, 'Darling, it must be terrible to be grown up!'如果有人问她多大了，她总是回答：“亲爱的，长大一定很可怕吧！”
