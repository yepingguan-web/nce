Reward for Virtue

新概念英语第2册 · 第 51 课 · 中英文对照课文

▶ 点读 / 听力练习
  1. Lesson 51第51课
  2. Reward for Virtue美德的报酬
  3. Why did Hugh's diet not work?为什么休的节食计划没有效果呢？
  4. My friend, Hugh, has always been fat, but things got so bad recently that he decided to go on a diet.我的朋友休一直都很胖。但最近情况变得非常糟糕，以至于他决定开始节食。
  5. He began his diet a week ago.他一周前开始节食了。
  6. First of all, he wrote out a long list of all the foods which were forbidden.首先，他列出了一份长长的清单，上面列出了所有被禁止食用的食物。
  7. The list included most of the things Hugh loves: butter, potatoes, rice, beer, milk, chocolate, and sweets.这份清单上列出了休最喜欢的大多数东西。黄油、土豆、米饭、啤酒、牛奶、巧克力和糖果。
  8. Yesterday I paid him a visit.昨天我去拜访了他。
  9. I rang the bell and was not surprised to see that Hugh was still as fat as ever.我按响了门铃，看到休还是和以前一样胖，并没有感到惊讶。
  10. He led me into his room and hurriedly hid a large parcel under his desk.他把我带进了他的房间，然后匆忙地把一个大包裹藏在了桌子下面。
  11. It was obvious that he was very embarrassed.很明显，他感到非常尴尬。
  12. When I asked him what he was doing, he smiled guiltily and then put the parcel on the desk.当我问他正在做什么时，他内疚地笑了笑，然后把包裹放在了桌子上。
  13. He explained that his diet was so strict that he had to reward himself occasionally.他解释说，他的饮食控制非常严格，因此偶尔需要奖励自己一下。
  14. Then he showed me the contents of the parcel.然后他向我展示了包裹里的物品。
  15. It contained five large bars of chocolate and three bags of sweets!里面是五块大巧克力和三袋糖果！
