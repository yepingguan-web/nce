The Future

新概念英语第2册 · 第 60 课 · 中英文对照课文

▶ 点读 / 听力练习
  1. Lesson 60第60课
  2. The Future未来
  3. Does what Madam Bellinsky said come true?贝尔林斯基夫人说的话会成真吗？
  4. At a village fair, I decided to visit a fortune-teller called Madam Bellinsky.在一个乡村集市上，我决定去拜访一位名叫贝林斯基夫人的算命师。
  5. I went into her tent and she told me to sit down.我走进她的帐篷，她让我坐下。
  6. After I had given her some money, she looked into a crystal ball and said:在我给了她一些钱之后，她看了看那个水晶球，然后说道：
  7. 'A relation of yours is coming to see you.你的一个亲戚要来看你。
  8. She will be arriving this evening and intends to stay for a few days.她将于今晚到达，并打算在这里待上几天。
  9. The moment you leave this tent, you will get a big surprise.你一离开这个帐篷，就会大吃一惊。
  10. A woman you know well will rush towards you.一个你非常熟悉的女人会冲向你。
  11. She will speak to you and then she will lead you away from this place. That is all.'她会和你说话，然后带你离开这个地方。就这些。
  12. As soon as I went outside, I forgot all about Madam Bellinsky because my wife hurried towards me.我一走到外面，就把贝林斯基夫人忘得一干二净了，因为我的妻子正朝我匆匆走来。
  13. 'Where have you been hiding?' she asked impatiently.你跑哪儿去了？她不耐烦地问道。
  14. 'Your sister will be here in less than an hour and we must be at the station to meet her. We are late already.'你妹妹将在一小时内到达，我们必须赶到车站去接她。我们已经迟到了。
  15. As she walked away, I followed her out of the fair.她转身走开时，我便跟着她离开了集市。
