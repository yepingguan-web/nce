An Exciting Trip

新概念英语第2册 · 第 4 课 · 中英文对照课文

▶ 点读 / 听力练习
  1. Lesson 4第4课
  2. An Exciting Trip激动人心的旅行
  3. First listen and then answer the question.先听录音，然后回答问题。
  4. Why is Tim finding this trip exciting?为什么蒂姆觉得这次旅行如此令人兴奋呢？
  5. I have just received a letter from my brother, Tim.我刚刚收到了我哥哥蒂姆的一封信。
  6. He is in Australia.他在澳大利亚。
  7. He has been there for six months.他已经在那里待了六个月了。
  8. Tim is an engineer.蒂姆是一名工程师。
  9. He is working for a big firm and he has already visited a great number of different places in Australia.他在一家大公司工作，并且已经去过澳大利亚的许多不同地方。
  10. He has just bought an Australian car and has gone to Alice Springs, a small town in the centre of Australia.他刚买了一辆澳大利亚产的汽车，然后去了爱丽丝泉（Alice Springs）。澳大利亚中部的一个小镇。
  11. He will soon visit Darwin.他很快将会去达尔文。
  12. From there, he will fly to Perth.从那里，他将飞往珀斯。
  13. My brother has never been abroad before, so he is finding this trip very exciting.我哥哥以前从未出过国。所以他觉得这次旅行非常令人兴奋。
