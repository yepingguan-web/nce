Sold Out

新概念英语第2册 · 第 19 课 · 中英文对照课文

▶ 点读 / 听力练习
  1. Lesson 19第19课
  2. Sold Out票已售完
  3. When will the writer see the play?作者什么时候能看到这部剧呢？
  4. 'The play may begin at any moment,' I said.“演出随时可能开始，”我说。
  5. 'It may have begun already,' Susan answered.“可能已经开始了，”苏珊回答。
  6. I hurried to the ticket office.我赶紧跑向售票处。
  7. 'May I have two tickets please?' I asked.“请给我两张票，好吗？”我问。
  8. 'I'm sorry, we've sold out,' the girl said.“很抱歉，票已经卖完了，”女孩说。
  9. 'What a pity!' Susan exclaimed.“真遗憾！”苏珊感叹道。
  10. Just then, a man hurried to the ticket office.就在这时，一个男人匆匆跑到了售票处。
  11. 'Can I return these two tickets?' he asked.“我可以退掉这两张票吗？”他问道。
  12. 'Certainly,' the girl said.“当然可以，”女孩回答。
  13. I went back to the ticket office at once.我立刻又回到了售票处。
  14. 'Could I have those two tickets please?' I asked.“请把那两张票给我，好吗？”我问。
  15. 'Certainly,' the girl said,“当然可以，”女孩说道。
  16. 'but they're for next Wednesday's performance.但这是下周三演出的票。
  17. Do you still want them?'你还要吗？
  18. 'I might as well have them,' I said sadly.“那我就要了吧，”我沮丧地说。
