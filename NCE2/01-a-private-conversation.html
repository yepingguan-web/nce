A Private Conversation

新概念英语第2册 · 第 1 课 · 中英文对照课文

▶ 点读 / 听力练习
  1. Lesson 1第1课
  2. A Private Conversation私人谈话
  3. Why did the writer complain to the people behind him?为什么作者会向他身后的人抱怨呢？
  4. Last week I went to the theatre.上周我去剧院看戏。
  5. I had a very good seat.我的座位非常好。
  6. The play was very interesting.这出戏非常有趣。
  7. I did not enjoy it.我并不喜欢它。
  8. A young man and a young woman were sitting behind me.一个年轻男子和一个年轻女子坐在我后面。
  9. They were talking loudly.他们说话的声音很大。
  10. I got very angry.我非常生气。
  11. I could not hear the actors.我听不到演员们的声音。
  12. I turned round.我转过身来。
  13. I looked at the man and the woman angrily.我愤怒地看着那个男人和那个女人。
  14. They did not pay any attention.他们根本没有理会。
  15. In the end, I could not bear it.最后，我实在无法忍受下去了。
  16. I turned round again.我又转过身来。
  17. 'I can't hear a word!' I said angrily.“我一个字都听不见！”我愤怒地说道。
  18. 'It's none of your business,' the young man said rudely.“这不关你的事。”那个年轻人粗鲁地说道。
  19. 'This is a private conversation!'“这可是私人谈话！”
