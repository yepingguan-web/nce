Out of Control

新概念英语第2册 · 第 86 课 · 中英文对照课文

▶ 点读 / 听力练习
  1. Lesson 86第86课
  2. Out of Control失控
  3. What was the danger?危险是什么？
  4. As the man tried to swing the speedboat round, the steering wheel came away in his hands.当那个男人试图调转快艇时，方向盘突然从他手中脱落了。
  5. He waved desperately to his companion, who had been water skiing for the last fifteen minutes.他拼命地向他的同伴挥手，而他的同伴已经滑水滑了十五分钟了。
  6. Both men had hardly had time to realize what was happening when they were thrown violently into the sea.两人几乎还没来得及反应发生了什么，就被猛地抛进了海里。
  7. The speedboat had struck a buoy, but it continued to move very quickly across the water.快艇撞到了一个浮标上，但仍然以极快的速度继续在水面上行驶。
  8. Both men had just begun to swim towards the shore, when they noticed with dismay that the speedboat was moving in a circle.两人刚刚开始朝岸边游去，就惊恐地发现快艇正在打转。
  9. It now came straight towards them at tremendous speed.现在，快艇正以惊人的速度朝他们直冲过来。
  10. In less than a minute, it roared past them only a few feet away.不到一分钟的时间，它就从他们身边呼啸而过，距离他们只有几英尺远。
  11. After it had passed, they swam on as quickly as they could because they knew that the boat would soon return.快艇驶过后，他们拼命地继续游动，因为他们知道快艇很快就会再次回来。
  12. They had just had enough time to swim out of danger when the boat again completed a circle.他们刚好来得及游出危险区域，这时快艇又转了一圈。
  13. On this occasion, however, it had slowed down considerably.然而这一次，它的速度明显慢了很多。
  14. The petrol had nearly all been used up.汽油几乎已经用完了。
  15. Before long, the noise dropped completely and the boat began to drift gently across the water.没过多久，引擎声完全消失了，小船开始在水面上缓缓漂动。
