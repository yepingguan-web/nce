Do You Speak English

新概念英语第2册 · 第 14 课 · 中英文对照课文

▶ 点读 / 听力练习
  1. Lesson 14第14课
  2. Do You Speak English你会讲英语吗？
  3. Did the young man speak English?那个年轻人会说英语吗？
  4. I had an amusing experience last year.去年我有一段非常有趣的经历。
  5. After I had left a small village in the south of France,我继续开车驶向下一个城镇。
  6. I drove on to the next town.我继续开车前往下一个城镇。
  7. On the way, a young man waved to me.路上，一个年轻人向我招手。
  8. I stopped and he asked me for a lift.我停下车，他问我能否搭个便车。
  9. As soon as he had got into the car,他一上车，
  10. I said good morning to him in French and he replied in the same language.我用法语向他道了早安，他也用法语回应了我。
  11. Apart from a few words,除了几个单词之外，
  12. I do not know any French at all.我完全不会说法语。
  13. Neither of us spoke during the journey.在旅途中，我们俩都没有说话。
  14. I had nearly reached the town, when the young man suddenly said, very slowly,我快到镇上的时候，那个年轻人突然非常缓慢地说：
  15. 'Do you speak English?'“你会说英语吗？”
  16. As I soon learnt, he was English himself!很快我就发现，他本人就是英国人！
