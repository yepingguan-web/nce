One Good Turn Deserves Another

新概念英语第2册 · 第 11 课 · 中英文对照课文

▶ 点读 / 听力练习
  1. Lesson 11第11课
  2. One Good Turn Deserves Another礼尚往来
  3. Who paid for Tony's dinner?谁付了托尼晚餐的钱？
  4. I was having dinner at a restaurant when Tony Steele came in.我正在餐厅吃晚饭，这时托尼·斯蒂尔走了进来。
  5. Tony worked in a lawyer's office years ago, but he is now working at a bank.托尼几年前在一家律师事务所工作过。但他现在在一家银行工作。
  6. He gets a good salary, but he always borrows money from his friends and never pays it back.他的薪水很高。但他总是向朋友借钱，却从不还钱。
  7. Tony saw me and came and sat at the same table.托尼看到我后，走过来坐在了我的旁边。
  8. He has never borrowed money from me.他从来没有向我借过钱。
  9. While he was eating, I asked him to lend me twenty pounds.他正在吃饭的时候，我请他借给我二十英镑。
  10. To my surprise, he gave me the money immediately.令我惊讶的是，他立刻就把钱给了我。
  11. 'I have never borrowed any money from you,'“我从来没有向你借过任何钱。”
  12. Tony said, 'so now you can pay for my dinner!'托尼说：“现在你可以付我晚餐的钱了！”
