The Last One

新概念英语第2册 · 第 78 课 · 中英文对照课文

▶ 点读 / 听力练习
  1. Lesson 78第78课
  2. The Last One最后一支吗？
  3. For how long did the writer give up smoking?作者戒烟坚持了多久？
  4. After reading an article entitled 'Cigarette Smoking and Your Health' I lit a cigarette to calm my nerves.在读完一篇题为《吸烟与健康》的文章后，我点燃了一支烟来缓解自己的紧张情绪。
  5. I smoked with concentration and pleasure as I was sure that this would be my last cigarette.我专注而愉悦地抽着这支烟，因为我知道这将是我的最后一支烟了。
  6. For a whole week I did not smoke at all and during this time my wife suffered terribly.整整一个星期里，我都没有抽烟；而在这期间，我的妻子却承受了极大的痛苦。
  7. I had all the usual symptoms of someone giving up smoking: a bad temper and an enormous appetite.我出现了所有典型的戒烟后症状：脾气变得暴躁，食欲变得异常旺盛。
  8. My friends kept on offering me cigarettes and cigars.我的朋友们一直不停地给我提供香烟和雪茄。
  9. They made no effort to hide their amusement whenever I produced a packet of sweets from my pocket.每当我从口袋里掏出一包糖果时，他们总是毫不掩饰自己的好笑。
  10. After seven days of this I went to a party.这样过了七天之后，我去参加了一个聚会。
  11. Everybody around me was smoking and I felt extremely uncomfortable.我周围的每个人都在抽烟，这让我感到非常不舒服。
  12. When my old friend Brian urged me to accept a cigarette, it was more than I could bear.当我的老朋友布莱恩坚持要我抽一支烟时，我真的再也无法忍受了。
  13. I took one guiltily, lit it and smoked with satisfaction.我带着一丝内疚感拿了一支烟，点燃它后，心满意足地抽了起来。
  14. My wife was delighted that things had returned to normal once more.我的妻子很高兴，因为一切终于恢复了正常。
  15. Anyway, as Brian pointed out, it is the easiest thing in the world to give up smoking.不管怎样，正如布莱恩指出的那样，戒烟是世界上最容易的事情。
  16. He himself has done it lots of times!他自己也做过很多次了！
