It Could be Worse

新概念英语第2册 · 第 24 课 · 中英文对照课文

▶ 点读 / 听力练习
  1. Lesson 24第24课
  2. It Could be Worse可能更糟
  3. Had the writer's money been stolen?那位作家的钱被偷了吗？
  4. I entered the hotel manager's office and sat down.我走进酒店经理的办公室，坐了下来。
  5. I had just lost ￡50 and I felt very upset.我刚刚损失了50英镑，感到非常沮丧。
  6. 'I left the money in my room,' I said, 'and it's not there now. '“我把钱放在房间里了，但现在钱不见了。”我说。
  7. The manager was sympathetic, but he could do nothing.经理很同情我，但他无能为力。
  8. 'Everyone's losing money these days,' he said.“如今大家都在丢钱，”他说。
  9. He started to complain about this wicked world but was interrupted by a knock at the door.他开始抱怨这个邪恶的世界，但被一阵敲门声打断了。
  10. A girl came in and put an envelope on his desk.一个女孩走了进来，把一个信封放在他的桌子上。
  11. It contained ￡50.信封里装着50英镑。
  12. 'I found this outside this gentleman's room,' she said.“我在这位先生的房间外面发现了这个。”她说。
  13. 'Well,' I said to the manager,'there is still some honesty in this world!'“嗯，”我对经理说，“这个世界上还是有一些诚实的人的！”
