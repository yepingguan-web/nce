A Fantasy

新概念英语第2册 · 第 95 课 · 中英文对照课文

▶ 点读 / 听力练习
  1. Lesson 95第95课
  2. A Fantasy纯属虚构
  3. Why was the Ambassador particularly lucky?为什么这位大使特别幸运呢？
  4. When the Ambassador of Escalopia returned home for lunch, his wife got a shock.当埃斯卡洛皮亚大使回家吃午饭时，他的妻子感到非常震惊。
  5. He looked pale and his clothes were in a frightful state.他看起来脸色苍白，衣服也破烂不堪。
  6. 'What has happened?' she asked.“发生了什么事？”她问道。
  7. 'How did your clothes get into such a mess?'“你的衣服怎么会弄得这么乱糟糟的？”
  8. 'A fire extinguisher, my dear,' answered the Ambassador drily.“是灭火器，亲爱的，”大使冷冷地回答道。
  9. 'University students set the Embassy on fire this morning.'“今天早上，大学生们把大使馆给点着了。”
  10. 'Good heavens!' exclaimed his wife.“我的天啊！”他的妻子惊呼道。
  11. 'And where were you at the time?'“那你当时在哪里？”
  12. 'I was in my office as usual,' answered the Ambassador.“我像往常一样在办公室里，”大使回答道。
  13. 'The fire broke out in the basement.“火是从地下室烧起来的。”
  14. I went down immediately, of course, and that fool, Horst, aimed a fire extinguisher at me.我立刻就下去了，当然了；还有那个傻瓜，霍斯特……“把灭火器对准了我。”
  15. He thought I was on fire.他以为我着火了。
  16. I must definitely get that fellow posted.'“我一定要把那个家伙调走。”
  17. The Ambassador's wife went on asking questions, when she suddenly noticed a big hole in her husband's hat.大使的妻子不停地提问。突然，她发现丈夫的帽子上有一个大洞。
  18. 'And how can you explain that?' she asked.“那你又该如何解释这件事呢？”她问道。
  19. 'Oh, that,' said the Ambassador.“哦，那个……”大使说道。
  20. 'Someone fired a shot through my office window.“有人朝我的办公室窗户开了一枪。”
  21. Accurate, don't you think?“非常准确，不是吗？”
  22. Fortunately, I wasn't wearing it at the time.幸运的是，当时我并没有戴着它。
  23. If I had been, I would not have been able to get home for lunch.'“如果我戴着它，我就没法回家吃午饭了。”
