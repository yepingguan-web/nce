Hurry Up

新概念英语第1册 · 第 43-44 课 · 中英文对照课文

▶ 点读 / 听力练习
  1. Lesson 43第43课
  2. Hurry up!快点！
  3. Listen to the tape then answer this question.请听录音，然后回答这个问题。
  4. How do you know Sam doesn't make the tea very often?你怎么知道山姆不经常泡茶的？
  5. Can you make the tea, Sam?你能泡茶吗，山姆？
  6. Yes, of course I can, Penny.当然能，佩妮。
  7. Is there any water in this kettle?这个水壶里有水吗？
  8. Yes, there is.有。
  9. Where's the tea?茶在哪里？
  10. It's over there, behind the teapot.就在那儿，茶壶后面。
  11. Can you see it?你能看到它吗？
  12. I can see the teapot, but I can't see the tea.我能看到茶壶，但看不到茶。
  13. There it is!在那儿呢！
  14. It's in front of you!它就在你面前！
  15. Ah yes, I can see it now.啊，对，我现在看到了。
  16. Where are the cups?杯子在哪里？
  17. There are some in the cupboard.橱柜里有几个。
  18. Can you find them?你能找到它们吗？
  19. Yes. Here they are.能。在这儿呢。
  20. Hurry up, Sam!快点，萨姆！
  21. The kettle's boiling!水壶已经烧开了！
