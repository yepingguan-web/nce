A Walk Through the Woods

新概念英语第1册 · 第 143-144 课 · 中英文对照课文

▶ 点读 / 听力练习
  1. Lesson 143第143课
  2. A walk through the woods在树林中散步
  3. Listen to the tape then answer this question.先听这段录音，然后再回答这个问题。
  4. What was so funny about the words on the sign?那个招牌上的文字有什么好笑的呢？
  5. I live in a very old town which is surrounded by beautiful woods.我住在一个非常古老的城镇里，这个城镇被美丽的树林环绕着。
  6. It is a famous beauty spot.这是一个著名的风景区。
  7. On Sundays, hundreds of people come from the city to see our town and to walk through the woods.每到周日，都会有数百人从城里来到我们这个小镇，然后漫步在树林里。
  8. Visitors have been asked to keep the woods clean and tidy.游客们被要求保持树林的整洁。
  9. Litter baskets have been placed under the trees, but people still throw their rubbish everywhere.虽然树底下已经放置了垃圾篓，人们仍然把垃圾扔得到处都是。
  10. Last Wednesday, I went for a walk in the woods.上周三，我去树林里散步了。
  11. What I saw made me very sad.我所看到的一切让我感到非常难过。
  12. I counted seven old cars and three old refrigerators.我数了七辆旧汽车和三台旧冰箱。
  13. The little baskets were empty and the ground was covered with pieces of paper, cigarette ends, old tyres, empty bottles and rusty tins.那些小篮子都是空的，地上到处是纸片、烟头、旧轮胎、空瓶子以及生锈的铁罐。
  14. Among the rubbish, I found a sign which said, 'Anyone who leaves litter in these woods will be prosecuted!'在那些垃圾中，我发现了一张告示，上面写着：“任何在这些树林里乱扔垃圾的人都将受到法律追究！”
