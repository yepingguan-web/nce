Sensational News

新概念英语第1册 · 第 133-134 课 · 中英文对照课文

▶ 点读 / 听力练习
  1. Lesson 133第133课
  2. Sensational news!惊人的新闻！
  3. Listen to the tape then answer this question.先听这段录音，然后再回答这个问题。
  4. What reasons did Karen Marsh give for wanting to retire?凯伦·马什提出了哪些想要退休的理由？
  5. Have you just made a new film, Miss Marsh?马什小姐，您刚刚完成了一部新电影的制作吗？
  6. Yes, I have.是的。
  7. Are you going to make another?您还准备再拍一部吗？
  8. No, I'm not.不，不拍了。
  9. I'm going to retire.我打算退休了。
  10. I feel very tired.我感到非常疲倦。
  11. I don't want to make another film for a long time.我很长时间都不想再拍电影了。
  12. Let's buy a newspaper, Liz.我们买份报纸吧，莉兹。
  13. Listen to this!你听听这个！
  14. 'Karen Marsh: Sensational News!凯伦·马什：轰动性新闻！
  15. By our reporter, Alan Jones.本报记者艾伦·琼斯报道。
  16. Karen Marsh arrived at London Airport today.凯伦·马什今天抵达了伦敦机场。
  17. She was wearing a blue dress and a mink coat.她穿着一条蓝色的连衣裙和一件貂皮大衣。
  18. She told me she had just made a new film.她告诉我她刚刚完成了一部新电影的制作。
  19. she said she was not going to make another.她说她不会再拍了。
  20. She said she was going to retire.她说她打算退休了。
  21. She told reporters she felt very tired and didn't want to make another film for a long time.'她对记者们表示，她感到非常疲惫，暂时不想再拍电影了。
  22. I wonder why!真不知道为什么！
