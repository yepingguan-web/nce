A True Story

新概念英语第1册 · 第 119-120 课 · 中英文对照课文

▶ 点读 / 听力练习
  1. Lesson 119第119课
  2. A true story一个真实的故事
  3. Listen to the tape then answer this question.先听这段录音，然后再回答这个问题。
  4. Who called out to the thieves in the dark?是谁在黑暗中向那些小偷喊叫的？
  5. Do you like stories?你喜欢故事吗？
  6. I want to tell you a true story.我想告诉你一个真实的故事。
  7. It happened to a friend of mine a year ago.这件事发生在我一个朋友身上，是在一年前。
  8. While my friend, George, was reading in bed, two thieves climbed into his kitchen.当我的朋友乔治在床上读书的时候，两个小偷爬进了他的厨房。
  9. After they had entered the house, they went into the dining room.他们进入房子后，他们走进了餐厅。
  10. It was very dark, so they turned on a torch.天非常黑，于是他们打开了一个手电筒。
  11. Suddenly, they heard a voice behind them.突然，他们听到身后传来一个声音。
  12. 'What's up? What's up?' someone called.“怎么了？出什么事了？”有人喊道。
  13. The thieves dropped the torch and ran away as quickly as they could.小偷们扔掉了手电筒，然后飞快地逃跑了。
  14. George heard the noise and came downstairs quickly.乔治听到了那个声音，立刻快步下楼去了。
  15. He turned on the light, but he couldn't see anyone.他打开了灯，却看不到任何人。
  16. The thieves had already gone.小偷们已经逃走了。
  17. But George's parrot, Henry, was still there.但是乔治的鹦鹉亨利还在那里。
  18. What's up, George? he called.“怎么了，乔治？”他问道。
  19. 'Nothing, Henry,' George said and smiled.“没什么，亨利。”乔治微笑着说。
  20. 'Go back to sleep.'回去睡觉吧。
