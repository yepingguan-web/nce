The Weekend

新概念英语第1册 · 第 67-68 课 · 中英文对照课文

▶ 点读 / 听力练习
  1. Lesson 67第67课
  2. The weekend周末
  3. Listen to the tape then answer this question.先听录音，然后回答这个问题：
  4. What are the Johnsons going to do at the weekend?约翰逊一家周末打算做什么？
  5. Hello. Were you at the butcher's?你好。你去过肉店吗？
  6. Yes, I was.是的，我去了。
  7. Were you at the butcher's, too?你也去肉店了吗？
  8. No, I wasn't.没有，我没去。
  9. I was at the greengrocer's.没有，我去的是蔬菜店。
  10. How's Jimmy today?吉米今天怎么样？
  11. He's very well, thank you.他很好，谢谢。
  12. Was he absent from school last week?他上周缺课了吗？
  13. Yes, he was.是的，他缺课了。
  14. He was absent on Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday and Thursday.他周一、周二、周三和周四都缺席了。
  15. How are you all keeping?大家最近怎么样？过得还好吗？
  16. Very well, thank you.好的，谢谢您。
  17. We're going to spend three days in the country!我们打算在乡下待三天！
  18. We're going to stay at my mother's for the weekend.我们这个周末将住在妈妈家。
  19. Friday, Saturday and Sunday in the country!周五、周六和周日都在乡下！
  20. Aren't you lucky!你真幸运啊！
