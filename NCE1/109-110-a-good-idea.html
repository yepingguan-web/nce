A Good Idea

新概念英语第1册 · 第 109-110 课 · 中英文对照课文

▶ 点读 / 听力练习
  1. Lesson 109第109课
  2. A good idea一个好主意
  3. Listen to the tape then answer this question.先听录音，然后回答这个问题：
  4. What does Jane have with her coffee?简喝咖啡时带了什么？
  5. Shall I make some coffee, Jane?“我给你煮点咖啡吧，简？”
  6. That's a good idea, Charlotte.“那是个好主意，夏洛特。”
  7. It's ready.“咖啡煮好了。”
  8. Do you want any milk?“你要加牛奶吗？”
  9. Just a little, please.“请加一点点。”
  10. What about some sugar?“那加点糖怎么样？”
  11. Two teaspoonfuls?“两茶匙？”
  12. No, less than that.“不，少一点就好。”
  13. One and a half teaspoonfuls, please.请给我一茶匙半。
  14. That's enough for me.对我来说，这就够了。
  15. That was very nice.这咖啡真不错。
  16. Would you like some more?您还要再来一些吗？
  17. Yes, please.好的，再来一点。
  18. I'd like a cigarette, too.我也想抽根烟。
  19. May I have one?我可以要一个吗？
  20. Of course.当然。
  21. I think there are a few in that box.我想那个盒子里有几支烟。
  22. I'm afraid it's empty.恐怕它是空的。
  23. What a pity!真遗憾！
  24. It doesn't matter.没关系。
  25. Have a biscuit instead.“那就吃块饼干吧。”
  26. Eat more and smoke less!“多吃点，少抽烟！”
  27. That's very good advice!这真是很好的建议！
