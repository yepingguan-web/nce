The Man in a Hat

新概念英语第1册 · 第 121-122 课 · 中英文对照课文

▶ 点读 / 听力练习
  1. Lesson 121第121课
  2. The man in a hat戴帽子的男子
  3. Listen to the tape then answer this question.请听录音，然后回答这个问题：
  4. Why didn't Caroline recognize the customer straight away?为什么卡罗琳没有立刻认出那位顾客？
  5. I bought two expensive dictionaries here half an hour ago, but I forgot to take them with me.半小时前我在这里买了两本昂贵的词典，但忘记带走了。
  6. Who served you, sir?先生，是谁接待您的？
  7. The lady who is standing behind the counter.是站在柜台后面的那位女士。
  8. Which books did you buy?您买了哪些书？
  9. The books which are on the counter.就是放在柜台上的那些书。
  10. Did you serve this gentleman half an hour ago, Caroline?卡罗琳，半小时前你接待过这位先生吗？
  11. He says he's the man who bought these books.他说他就是买这些书的人。
  12. I can't remember.我记不起来了。
  13. The man who I served was wearing a hat.我接待过的那位男士戴着帽子。
  14. Have you got a hat, sir?先生，您有帽子吗？
  15. Yes, I have.是的，我有。
  16. Would you put it on, please?请您戴上它好吗？
  17. All right.好的。
  18. Is this the man that you served, Caroline?卡罗琳，这就是你接待过的那位男士吗？
  19. Yes.是的。
  20. I recognize him now.我现在认出他来了。
