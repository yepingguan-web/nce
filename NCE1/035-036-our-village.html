Our Village

新概念英语第1册 · 第 35-36 课 · 中英文对照课文

▶ 点读 / 听力练习
  1. Lesson 35第35课
  2. Our village我们的村庄
  3. Listen to the tape then answer this question.先听这段录音，然后再回答这个问题。
  4. Are the children coming out of the park or going into it?孩子们是从公园里出来，还是正要进入公园呢？
  5. This is a photograph of our village.这是一张我们村庄的照片。
  6. Our village is in a valley.我们的村庄在一个山谷里。
  7. It is between two hills.它在两座小山之间。
  8. The village is on a river.这个村庄在一条河边。
  9. Here is another photograph of the village.这是另一张村庄的照片。
  10. My wife and I are walking along the banks of the river.我和我的妻子正在河边散步。
  11. We are on the left.我们在左边。
  12. There is a boy in the water.水里有一个男孩。
  13. He is swimming across the river.他正在游过这条河。
  14. Here is another photograph.这是另一张照片。
  15. This is the school building.这是学校大楼。
  16. It is beside a park.它在一个公园旁边。
  17. The park is on the right.公园在右边。
  18. Some children are coming out of the building.一些孩子正从大楼里走出来。
  19. Some of them are going into the park.其中一些人正前往公园。
