Roast Beef and Potatoes

  2. Roast beef and potatoes烤牛肉配土豆
  3. Listen to the tape then answer this question.听完录音后回答这个问题：
  4. Why is Carol disappointed?为什么卡罗尔感到失望？
  5. Hi, Carol! Where's Tom?嗨，Carol！Tom在哪儿？
  6. He's upstairs.他在楼上。
  7. He's having a bath.他正在洗澡。
  8. Tom!汤姆！
  9. Yes?怎么了？
  10. Sam's here.山姆来了。
  11. I'm nearly ready.我差不多准备好了。
  12. Hello, Sam.你好，山姆。
  13. Have a cigarette.来一支烟吧。
  14. No, thanks, Tom.不，谢谢，汤姆。
  15. Have a glass of whisky then.那就喝一杯威士忌吧。
  16. OK.好的。
  17. Thanks.谢谢。
  18. Is dinner ready, Carol?卡罗尔，晚餐准备好了吗？
  19. It's nearly ready.差不多准备好了。
  20. We can have dinner at seven o'clock.我们可以在七点钟吃晚饭。
  21. Sam and I had lunch together today.今天我和山姆一起吃了午饭。
  22. We went to a restaurant.我们去了餐厅。
  23. What did you have?你吃了什么？
  24. We had roast beef and potatoes.我们吃了烤牛肉和土豆。
  25. Oh!哦！
  26. What's the matter, Carol?怎么了，卡罗尔？
  27. Well, you're going to have roast beef and potatoes again tonight!嗯，今晚你又可以吃烤牛肉和土豆了！
