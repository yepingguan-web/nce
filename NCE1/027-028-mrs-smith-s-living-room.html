Mrs. Smith's Living Room

新概念英语第1册 · 第 27-28 课 · 中英文对照课文

▶ 点读 / 听力练习
  1. Lesson 27第27课
  2. Mrs. Smith's living room史密斯夫人的客厅
  3. Listen to the tape then answer this question.先听这段录音，然后再回答这个问题。
  4. Where are the books?书在哪里？
  5. Mrs. Smith's living room is large.史密斯太太的客厅非常大。
  6. There is a television in the room.房间里有一台电视机。
  7. The television is near the window.电视机放在窗户旁边。
  8. There are some magazines on the television.电视机上有几本杂志。
  9. There is a table in the room.房间里有一张桌子。
  10. There are some newspapers on the table.桌子上有一些报纸。
  11. There are some armchairs in the room.房间里有一些扶手椅。
  12. The armchairs are near the table.那些扶手椅都放在桌子旁边。
  13. There is a stereo in the room.房间里有一台立体声音响。
  14. The stereo is near the door.音响在门旁边。
  15. There are some books on the stereo.音响上有几本书。
  16. There are some pictures in the room.房间里有几幅画。
  17. The pictures are on the wall.画在墙上。
