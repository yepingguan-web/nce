A Trip to Australia

新概念英语第1册 · 第 123-124 课 · 中英文对照课文

▶ 点读 / 听力练习
  1. Lesson 123第123课
  2. A trip to Australia澳大利亚之旅
  3. Listen to the tape then answer this question.听完录音后，请回答这个问题：
  4. Who is the man with the beard?那个留着胡子的男人是谁？
  5. Look, Scott.看，斯科特。
  6. This is a photograph I took during my trip to Australia.这是我在澳大利亚旅行时拍的照片。
  7. Let me see it, Mike.让我看看，迈克。
  8. This is a good photograph.这张照片真不错！
  9. Who are these people?这些人是谁？
  10. They're people I met during the trip.他们是我在旅行中遇到的人。
  11. That's the ship we travelled on.那就是我们乘坐的船。
  12. What a beautiful ship!多漂亮的船啊！
  13. Who's this?这是谁？
  14. That's the man I told you about.就是我跟你提过的那个人。
  15. Remember?记得吗？
  16. Ah yes.啊，对。
  17. The one who offered you a job in Australia.就是那个在澳大利亚给你提供工作机会的人。
  18. That's right.没错。
  19. Who's this?这是谁？
  20. Guess!猜猜看！
  21. It's not you, is it?肯定不是你吧？
  22. That's right.没错。
  23. I grew a beard during the trip, but I shaved it off when I came home.我在旅途中留了胡子，但回家后就把它刮掉了。
  24. Why did you shave it off?你为什么要刮掉胡子呢？
  25. My wife didn't like it!我妻子不喜欢！
