A Lost Ship

新概念英语第3册 · 第 32 课 · 中英文对照课文

▶ 点读 / 听力练习
  1. Lesson 32第32课
  2. A lost ship一艘失踪的船只
  3. Did the crew of the Elkor find what they were looking for? Why?埃尔科号（Elkor）的船员们找到他们想要的东西了吗？为什么？
  4. The salvage operation had been a complete failure.这次打捞作业彻底失败了。
  5. The small ship, Elkor, which had been searching the Barents Sea for weeks, was on its way home.那艘名为“Elkor”的小船已经在巴伦支海搜寻了数周，现在正返回家中。
  6. A radio message from the mainland had been received by the ship's captain instructing him to give up the search.船长接收到了一则来自大陆的无线电消息，命令他停止搜索。
  7. The captain knew that another attempt would be made later, for the sunken ship he was trying to find had been carrying a precious cargo of gold bullion.船长知道，稍后还会有人再次尝试。因为他试图寻找的那艘沉船，当时正载着一批珍贵的黄金货物。
  8. Despite the message, the captain of the Elkor decided to try once more.尽管收到了那条消息，埃尔科号（Elkor）的船长还是决定再试一次。
  9. The sea bed was scoured with powerful nets and there was tremendous excitement on board when a chest was raised from the bottom.海床被强大的渔网彻底清理过；当一个箱子从海底被打捞上来时，船上的所有人都兴奋不已。
  10. Though the crew were at first under the impression that the lost ship had been found, the contents of the chest proved them wrong.虽然船员们最初以为那艘失踪的船只已经被找到了，箱子里的物品证明他们是错的。
  11. What they had in fact found was a ship which had been sunk many years before.他们实际上发现的是一艘多年前就已经沉没的船只。
  12. The chest contained the personal belongings of a seaman, Alan Fielding.这个箱子里装着水手艾伦·菲尔丁的个人物品。
  13. There were books, clothing and photographs, together with letters which the seaman had once received from his wife.那里有书籍、衣物和照片，还有一些信件——这些都是那位水手曾经从他的妻子那里收到的。
  14. The captain of the Elkor ordered his men to salvage as much as possible from the wreck.埃尔科号（Elkor）的船长命令他的船员们从残骸中尽可能多地抢救出有用的物品。
  15. Nothing of value was found, but the numerous items which were brought to the surface proved to be of great interest.没有发现任何有价值的东西。但是，那些被发掘出来的众多物品都引起了极大的兴趣。
  16. From a heavy gun that was raised, the captain realized that the ship must have been a cruiser.当那门大炮被抬起来时，船长意识到这艘船应该是一艘巡洋舰。
  17. In another chest which contained the belongings of a ship's officer, there was an unfinished letter which had been written on March 14th, 1943.在另一个箱子里，装着一名船员的个人物品。有一封未完成的信，写于1943年3月14日。
  18. The captain learnt from the letter that the name of the lost ship was the Karen.从这封信中，船长得知那艘失踪的船只的名字叫“Karen”。
  19. The most valuable find of all was the ship's log book, parts of which it was still possible to read.最珍贵的发现是那艘船的航海日志；其中的一部分内容仍然可以辨认出来。
  20. From this the captain was able to piece together all the information that had come to light.通过这些信息，船长得以整理出所有已经公开的事实和细节。
  21. The Karen had been sailing in a convoy to Russia when she was torpedoed by an enemy submarine.那艘名为“Karen”的船当时正与一支船队一起前往俄罗斯，途中被敌方潜艇发射的鱼雷击中。
  22. This was later confirmed by a naval official at the Ministry of Defence after the Elkor had returned home.这一事件在“Elkor”号返回国内后，得到了国防部海军官员的证实。
  23. All the items that were found were sent to the War Museum.船上发现的所有物品都被送到了战争博物馆。
